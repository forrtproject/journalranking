Science is a cumulative endeavor. To understand the robustness of our literature, we must track not only what is replicated, but where these conversations happen. This living report dynamically connects to the FORRT Library of Reproduction and Replication Attempts (FLoRA) to provide an up-to-date overview of replication outcomes across academic journals.

Publishers of Replications

Which journals are actively publishing replication attempts? The chart below highlights the top publishers. Note that this view includes large-scale replication attempts, where one paper with up to 160 replications is published at once and often without reference to the target studies.

The chart displays the Top 10 journals by default. NA means that the replication has not yet been published by a journal. Scroll your mouse wheel over the chart to pan down the list of journals.
